About Us - Food Waste Tracker

Our Mission

We are dedicated to reducing food waste and helping businesses track, manage, and minimize waste efficiently. Our platform provides smart analytics and actionable insights to prevent unnecessary food wastage.

How We Help

Our platform allows restaurants and food businesses to log their daily waste, analyze reports, and track progress. By understanding waste patterns, businesses can make informed decisions, saving money and contributing to a sustainable future.

Success Stories

✅ GreenLeaf Diner reduced food waste by 40% in just 3 months using our platform.
✅ FreshBites Cafe saved $500/month by identifying most wasted items and optimizing portions.
✅ Foodie Hub partnered with local NGOs to donate surplus edible food, reducing waste significantly.
